What is Lip Dub MICDS 2018?

This is a project to orchestrate a lip dub for MICDS in 2018. We plan to showcase the entire campus in the video, as well as some of the amazing groups we have on campus.

When will it happen?

The production date is March 16, 2018, the last school day before spring break.

Who Is Running This?

Sebastian Neumann is a ninth grader at MICDS, and as a new student he was amazed by all of the great facilities the school has. He wanted to show the rest of the St. Louis community (and beyond) all the wonderful things MICDS has to offer. Around the same time, a school in Kansas City, his home town, made a lip dub, and that spurred him into action. Now, he hopes he can show everyone how amazing it is to be a ram.
