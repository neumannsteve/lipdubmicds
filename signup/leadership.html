Sign Up for Leadership Team

To sign up for a chance at being on the leadership team, please fill out the following form. There are two types of leadership position available: a single Assistant Director and multiple co-leaders. Serving in either position will get you five service hours!

Assistant Director

The assistant director will help Sebastian coordinate the co-leaders, talk to administration, and handle the overall details of the lip dub.

Co-Leaders

The co-leaders will work in pairs to coordinate a certain section of the lip dub, handling logistics and other matters in the context of that section such as choosing performers, choosing songs, handling decorations, and so forth.
